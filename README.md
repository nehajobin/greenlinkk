Project Description

GreenLink is a student-driven sustainability platform that measures, ranks, and improves environmental responsibility across college campuses. It allows students and authorities to track eco-friendly initiatives, share updates, and compete to make their campus the greenest, promoting transparency and measurable impact.

Problem Statement

Many college campuses claim to support sustainability, but there is no transparent, measurable, and student-driven system to track environmental initiatives.
As a result:

Sustainability efforts are often performative rather than accountable

Students have little visibility into campus environmental impact

Colleges lack motivation to improve beyond superficial measures

GreenLink solves this by creating a centralized, measurable, and competitive platform for campuses to track, share, and improve their green initiatives.
